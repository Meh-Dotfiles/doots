 A simple startpage with a search bar for quick results when I open my browser. Hovering at the bottom of the page shows hardcoded bookmarks to websites I visit often.

Custom search usage: end your query with a flag to search a specific site, ex. to search YouTube for "cat videos" enter "cat videos!y"
